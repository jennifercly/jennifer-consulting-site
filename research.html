Peer-Reviewed Publications

Journal Articles

Published in internationally recognised, peer-reviewed journals on corporate governance and management.

Gender in Management: An International Journal
"Board Involvement in Strategic Decision-Making and Big Data Analytics Investments — The Moderating Role of Gender Diversity" Forthcoming

Lead Author  ·  Peer-reviewed

Examines how gender diversity on corporate boards moderates the relationship between board involvement in strategic decision-making and investments in big data analytics. Provides empirical evidence for the strategic value of diverse boards.

Journal of General Management · Mar 12, 2024
"Gender Diversity, Education Diversity, and Big Data Analytics Investments — A Test of Moderation"

Lead Author  ·  DOI: 10.1177/03063070231218312

Tests the moderating effects of board gender diversity and education diversity on the relationship between board characteristics and big data analytics investment decisions. Contributes to the growing body of evidence on the business case for diverse boards.

MATEC Web of Conferences · Apr 17, 2023
"Board Capital and Firm Performance"

Lead Author

Explores the relationship between board capital — the aggregate human and social capital held by board directors — and overall firm performance. Draws on data from listed companies to identify the composition characteristics most associated with superior outcomes.

Research Themes

Areas of Academic Focus

Eight years of research concentrated in four interconnected areas of corporate governance.

🏛

Board Capital & Effectiveness

What makes a board effective? My research explores how board composition, director experience, and human capital affect decision-making quality and firm performance. This underpins all my board assessment work.

👥

Gender Diversity in Management

The impact of female directors on strategic decision-making, risk identification, and firm outcomes. Evidence consistently shows diverse boards make better decisions — my research quantifies how and why.

💡

Big Data & Strategic Oversight

How boards oversee and govern technology investments and data-driven strategy. Increasingly important as boards navigate digital transformation and AI — an area of growing practical relevance.

📐

Quantitative Research Methods

Advanced statistical analysis including panel data analysis, PLS-SEM modelling, and econometric techniques. My recommendations are data-backed — not just informed by case studies or professional experience.
