Overview

Who I Am

I'm a corporate governance expert helping Singapore's boards strengthen effectiveness, reach compliance targets, and build diverse leadership. My work combines academic research rigour with practical consulting experience, bridging the gap between governance theory and business reality.

With a PhD in Corporate Governance and over 8 years of research focused on board effectiveness and gender diversity, I understand both the why (research evidence) and the how (practical implementation). I've published research in leading academic journals while simultaneously working directly with Singapore's boards on real-world governance challenges.

I believe effective governance isn't just about compliance — it's about boards that make better decisions, develop leadership pipelines, attract investment, and create sustainable value. My mission is to help boards move from "compliance checkbox" to "governance excellence."

Why Governance Matters

The Board Governance Imperative

Your board shapes your organisation's future. After 8+ years of research, the evidence is clear:

Strong boards achieve:

  • Better strategic decisions (diverse perspectives, healthy debate)
  • More effective risk management (catching what others miss)
  • Greater investor confidence and capital attraction
  • Stronger talent retention and leadership pipelines
  • Regulatory compliance and reputational protection
  • Sustainable long-term value creation

Weak governance leads to:

  • Poor strategic choices and missed opportunities
  • Unidentified and unmanaged risks
  • SGX regulatory problems and reputational damage
  • Leadership crises without succession plans
  • Investor scepticism and reduced access to capital
  • Talent drain from leadership pipeline failures
