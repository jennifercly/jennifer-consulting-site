"Good governance is not a compliance checkbox — it is the foundation upon which sustainable, high-performing organisations are built."

Jennifer Chong is a Singapore-based corporate governance consultant and PhD-qualified researcher specialising in board effectiveness, gender diversity, and governance frameworks for SGX-listed companies.

With deep expertise spanning board composition, strategic decision-making, and big data analytics investment, Jennifer works with boards, remuneration committees, and company secretaries to design governance structures that are not just compliant — but genuinely effective.

Her research, published in the Journal of General Management and MATEC Web of Conferences, provides the empirical backbone to her advisory practice — ensuring every recommendation is grounded in evidence, not intuition.
